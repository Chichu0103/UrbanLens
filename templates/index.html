Documentation

Please review the following articles to gain an understanding of the model and technology we are using.

Problem Statement

In rapidly growing cities, the distribution of green spaces versus non-green areas significantly impacts air quality, urban sustainability, and residents' health.

Read more

Models and Pipeline

Using IBM Granite for GeoSpatial AI and advanced time series models, UrbanLens analyses geospatial data to provide accurate predictions and metrics related to green spaces, urban growth, and environmental health.

Read more

Database Technology

UrbanLens leverages IBM Cloudant, a scalable NoSQL database, to store essential environmental and population data. Key components include: Geospatial Data,Population and Demographics,Oxygen Metrics and many more.
